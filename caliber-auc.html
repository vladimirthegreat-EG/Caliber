The Curriculum

What Teams Manage

Strategy & Leadership

Direction, alignment, competitive positioning

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Set company vision and strategic direction
  • Allocate resources across business units
  • Define competitive positioning by segment
  • Balance short-term profits vs long-term growth
  • Coordinate cross-functional decisions
  • Respond to market changes and competitor moves

Finance & Capital

Cash, debt, equity, risk management

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Manage cash flow and working capital
  • Raise capital through loans, bonds, or equity
  • Set dividend policy and shareholder returns
  • Hedge foreign exchange exposure
  • Monitor financial ratios and credit rating
  • Present proposals to the board of directors
  • Generate financial statements and reports

Human Resources

Hiring, training, productivity, morale

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Recruit workers, engineers, and supervisors
  • Choose recruitment tier (Basic, Premium, Executive)
  • Design training programs with fatigue management
  • Set compensation levels and salary structures
  • Configure benefits (health, retirement, PTO, stock options)
  • Monitor workforce morale and productivity
  • Manage turnover and retention strategies

Innovation & R&D

Product development, investment under uncertainty

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Develop new products for market segments
  • Set quality and feature targets
  • Invest in technology upgrades (Miniaturization, Battery, Display, AI)
  • Manage R&D budget allocation
  • File and maintain patents
  • Balance development speed vs product quality
  • Launch products at optimal timing

Operations & Supply

Capacity, efficiency, logistics

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Invest in factory efficiency (workers, machinery, engineers)
  • Purchase upgrades (Six Sigma, Automation, Green Energy, Robotics)
  • Manage production capacity and utilization
  • Choose shipping methods (Sea, Air, Land, Rail)
  • Source materials from regional suppliers
  • Monitor defect rates and quality control
  • Reduce CO2 emissions and improve ESG score

Marketing & Pricing

Segmentation, pricing, brand, demand

Click to explore

Key Decisions

  • Allocate advertising budget by channel (TV, Digital, Social, Print)
  • Target market segments (Budget, General, Enthusiast, Professional, Active)
  • Invest in brand building activities
  • Launch promotions (Discounts, Bundles, Loyalty programs)
  • Set product pricing strategy
  • Monitor brand awareness and customer satisfaction
  • Analyze market share by segment
The Process

The Simulation Flow

1

Form Teams

CEO, CFO, COO, CMO, HR Lead. Assign roles and align on strategy.

2

Decide

Products, pricing, hiring, investment, financing decisions each round.

3

Simulate

Market responds. Revenue, profit, market share calculated.

4

Adapt

Review results. Refine strategy. Outcompete rivals.

Repeat for 6-8 Rounds
